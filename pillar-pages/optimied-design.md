What is Website Design?

Website design is the process of creating the visual layout, user interface, and overall aesthetic of a website. It covers everything from color schemes and typography to navigation and user experience (UX).

Website Design vs. Web Development

While often confused, these are two distinct disciplines:

Website Design covers:

Layouts & wireframes

UI/UX elements

Fonts, colors, and imagery

Navigation

Web Development covers:

Coding & functionality

Database management

Server-side configuration
